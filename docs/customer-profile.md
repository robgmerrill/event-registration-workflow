# Customer Profile

## Customer

**TechForward Learning**

A growing professional training company that delivers instructor-led workshops to corporate customers.

---

## Industry

Education Technology (EdTech)

---

## Company Size

- 25 employees
- Operations team of 3
- Approximately 20–30 events each month

---

## Primary Stakeholders

### Event Coordinator

Responsible for managing registrations and attendee communication.

### Operations Manager

Needs visibility into registrations across all upcoming events.

### Training Manager

Needs confidence that attendees are registered correctly before each event begins.

---

## Current Process

The company currently manages registrations manually using:

- Email
- Shared spreadsheets
- Manual confirmation emails

---

## Current Challenges

- Duplicate data entry
- Manual confirmation emails
- Limited reporting
- No centralized dashboard
- Difficult to monitor registration volume

---

## Desired Outcome

Implement a lightweight workflow that:

- Collects registrations through a web form
- Stores registrations centrally
- Automatically generates confirmation messages
- Gives staff a dashboard for viewing registrations
- Provides basic operational metrics

---

## Success Criteria

The customer considers the implementation successful if:

- Registrations require no manual data entry
- Every attendee receives a consistent confirmation
- Operations staff can view registrations from one dashboard
- Event metrics are available without exporting spreadsheets
